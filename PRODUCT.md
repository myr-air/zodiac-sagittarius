# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Sagittarius serves group-trip organizers, travelers, and read-only viewers. They use the app while planning a shared trip, checking itinerary details, deciding who joins which activity, and keeping shared trip money understandable.

## Product Purpose

Sagittarius is a travel planning cockpit for group trips. Success means the group can see the plan, resolve decisions, track members, and manage shared costs without losing context across itinerary, people, and money.

## Brand Personality

Calm, practical, friendly. The product should feel trustworthy for long planning sessions while still carrying a light travel energy.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition inside the workspace, oversized heroes, decorative card stacks, purple-heavy prototype styling, one-note palettes, emoji-based controls, and dashboards that hide editing behind extra clicks.

## Design Principles

- Keep the planning cockpit dense but readable.
- Put the user's next money or trip decision near the relevant context.
- Use familiar app controls and stable table/list behavior.
- Let color clarify status, category, and action priority.
- Make mobile feel like a focused trip companion without sacrificing desktop operations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic controls, keyboard-visible focus states, readable contrast, stable responsive layouts, and restrained motion. Favor labels and icons that work for English and Thai text.
